# Introduction

Real-time ocean surface simulation has been featured in games such as
[Sea of Thieves]() and [Assassin's Creed: Black Flag](). Both of those games
were released in the 2010s, however the technology to simulate
the surface of the ocean goes back much further, to the late 1990s
and formally documented through Tessendorf's paper [Simulating Ocean
Water]().
